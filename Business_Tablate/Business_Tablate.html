About Mairelle's Gourmet Catering


Elevating Kobe Beef since 2003

Founded in Philippines in 2003, Mairelle's Gourmet Catering is synonymous with culinary sophistication and excellence. Chef Mairelle Tablate, a pioneer in gourmet cuisine, is renowned for her mastery in crafting exquisite dishes centered around the legendary Kobe beef. With a commitment to culinary artistry, Mairelle's Gourmet Catering delivers an unparalleled dining experience, showcasing the finest Kobe beef steaks to tantalize the most discerning palates.Seasonal Selections:

Seasonal Selections: Chef Mairelle sources only the highest quality Kobe beef, renowned for its exceptional marbling and unparalleled tenderness. Each dish is meticulously prepared to highlight the natural flavors of the beef, creating an unforgettable gastronomic journey. Complemented by seasonal ingredients and expertly paired flavors, Mairelle's Kobe beef steaks are a celebration of culinary excellence.
